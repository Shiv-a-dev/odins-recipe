Litti-Chokha's recipe

Litti (Stuffed Wheat Balls)

Ingredients for Dough:

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 tsp ajwain (carom seeds)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil (plus more for brushing)
  • Water as needed

Ingredients for Stuffing (Sattu Filling):

  • 1 cup sattu (roasted gram flour)
  • 2 tbsp mustard oil
  • 1 green chili (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 2‐3 crushed garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • 1 tsp lemon juice or dry mango powder
  • 1/2 tsp ajwain
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 small onion, chopped (optional)
  • 1 tsp pickle masala (optional)

Method for Litti:

  1. Knead soft dough using flour, ajwain, salt, ghee, and water. Rest 20 mins.
  2. Mix all stuffing ingredients well (keep it crumbly).
  3. Make dough balls, fill with stuffing, seal, and roll gently.
  4. Roast on gas, tandoor, oven, or tawa until golden and cooked.
  5. Brush or dip in ghee before serving.

Chokha (Mashed Veggies)

Ingredients:

  • 1 large brinjal (eggplant)
  • 2 medium tomatoes
  • 2 boiled potatoes
  • 4‐5 garlic cloves
  • 1‐2 green chilies
  • 1‐2 tbsp mustard oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves
  • 1 tsp lemon juice (optional)

Method for Chokha:

  1. Roast brinjal, tomatoes, garlic directly on flame or oven until skin is charred
  2. Peel, mash everything with boiled potatoes.
  3. Add chopped onion, chilies, salt, mustard oil, coriander leaves, lemon juice.
  4. Mix well and let flavors settle before serving.

Serve Hot:

  • Dip littis in ghee and serve with chokha.
  • Add sliced onions and pickles if desired.
