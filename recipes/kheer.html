Kheer's recipe

Ingredients

  • Full‐fat milk‐ 1 liter
  • Basmati rice‐ 1/4 cup (washed and soaked for 30 mins)
  • Sugar‐ 1/4 to 1/3 cup (adjust to taste)
  • Cardamom powder‐ 1/2 tsp
  • Chopped nuts almonds, cashews, pistachios‐ 2‐3 tbsp total
  • Raisins‐ 1 tbsp (optional)
  • Ghee‐ 1 tsp optional, for frying nuts
  • Saffron strands‐ a few optional, (soaked in 1 tbsp warm milk)

Methods

  1. Boil the milk in a heavy‐bottomed pan. Once it starts boiling, reduce the heat to low.
  2. Add the soaked rice to the milk. Stir gently.
  3. Let it simmer on low heat. Stir occasionally to avoid burning.
  4. Cook for 30‐40 minutes till the rice is soft and the milk thickens.
  5. Add sugar, stir well, and cook for another 5‐10 minutes.
  6. Add cardamom powder and saffron milk (if using).
  7. In a small pan, heat ghee and lightly fry the nuts and raisins. Add to the kheer.
  8. Stir everything well. Turn off the heat.

Serve

Hot or chilled, as you prefer. Garnish with extra nuts or a pinch of cardamom powder.
