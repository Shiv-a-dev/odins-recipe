Dosa's recipe

Ingredients

  • Raw rice‐2 cups.
  • Urad dal (split black gram) ‐ 1/2 cup
  • Fenugreek seeds ‐ 1 tsp
  • Poha (flattened rice) ‐ 2 tbsp (optional, for crispiness)
  • Salt ‐ to taste
  • Water ‐ as needed
  • Oil or ghee ‐ for greasing and drizzling

Methods

  1. Soaking:

    2. Wash and soak rice, urad dal, fenugreek seeds, and poha in water separately or together for 4‐6 hours.

  2. Grinding:

    3. Blend everything to a smooth batter using little water. Batter should be slightly thick but pourable.

    Mix well and transfer to a large bowl.

  3. Fermentation:

    4. Cover and let it ferment overnight (8‐12 hrs) in a warm place. It should rise and turn airy.

    Add salt and stir the batter gently before making dosa.

  4. Making Dosa:

    5. Heat a non/stick or cast/iron tava/pan on medium‐high heat.

    Pour a ladle of batter in the center and spread it in a circular motion to make a thin layer.

    Drizzle a few drops of oil/ghee around edges.

    Let it cook till golden and crispy. (No need to flip if it's thin.)

    Fold and serve!

Serve With

Coconut chutney, sambar, and potato masala (for masala dosa).
